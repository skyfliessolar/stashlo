💳 Stashlo — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 10 June 2026 · Operated by Stashlo Ltd, United Kingdom

Summary: We store the minimum data needed to run your loyalty wallet — your account details, loyalty cards, orders, and receipts you choose to save. We never sell your data. You can delete your account and data at any time.

1. Who we are

Stashlo ("we", "us") provides a digital loyalty wallet for customers and merchants in the UK. Contact: privacy@stashlo.app.

2. Data we collect

  • Account data: name, email address, phone number (optional), encrypted password (managed by Supabase Auth — we can never see it).
  • Loyalty data: loyalty cards you add, card numbers, points balances, your short Stashlo ID.
  • Location data (optional): only with your permission, to show nearby shops and remind you to use your loyalty card. You can disable this in your device settings at any time.
  • Orders & receipts: orders you place and receipt photos you choose to scan and save.
  • Payment metadata: card last-4 digits, network and expiry only. We never store full card numbers or CVV.
  • Messages: chats between you, merchants and our support team.

3. How we use your data

  • To operate your loyalty wallet, orders, rewards, and receipts.
  • To send you notifications you've opted into (offers, order updates, nearby reminders).
  • To prevent fraud and keep the platform secure.
  • If you join the Stashlo Credit waitlist, to contact you about that service before launch.

4. Legal bases (UK GDPR)

Performance of contract (running your wallet), consent (location, notifications, waitlist), and legitimate interests (security, fraud prevention).

5. Sharing

We share data only with: (a) merchants you interact with (your name, Stashlo ID and points at their shop); (b) our infrastructure providers (Supabase — database & authentication, GitHub Pages — hosting, Anthropic — AI assistant processing for merchant support replies); (c) authorities where legally required. We never sell personal data.

6. Storage & security

Data is stored with Supabase (encrypted at rest and in transit, EU/UK-compliant infrastructure). Passwords are hashed via Supabase Auth. Admin actions are audit-logged.

7. Retention

Account data is kept while your account is active. Saved receipts: 12 months on the free plan, or per your paid plan. You may request deletion at any time.

8. Your rights

Under UK GDPR you can access, correct, export, restrict or delete your data, and object to processing. Email privacy@stashlo.app. You may also complain to the ICO (ico.org.uk).

9. Children

Stashlo is not intended for children under 13. Stashlo Credit (when launched) will be 18+ only.

10. Changes

We will post updates to this page and notify you of material changes in-app.
