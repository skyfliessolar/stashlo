💳 Stashlo — Terms of Service

Effective date: 10 June 2026

1. The service

Stashlo is a digital loyalty wallet connecting customers and merchants. Loyalty points are issued by merchants and have no cash value. Orders placed through Stashlo are contracts between you and the merchant.

2. Accounts

You must provide accurate information and keep your password secure. One account per person. We may suspend accounts engaged in fraud or abuse.

3. Payments

Card details are tokenised; we store only last-4 digits, network and expiry. Job unlock fees (£1 single / £5 monthly) are non-refundable once admin approval is granted. Merchant subscription fees are billed per the plan selected.

4. Stashlo Credit

Stashlo Credit is not yet available. Any future credit product will be offered subject to status, UK FCA authorisation, separate terms, and affordability checks. Joining the waitlist creates no obligation on either side.

5. Merchant terms

Merchants are responsible for the accuracy of their products, prices, rewards and job posts, and for fulfilling orders and honouring rewards. Stashlo may restrict or suspend merchant accounts with unpaid fees or policy breaches.

6. Acceptable use
