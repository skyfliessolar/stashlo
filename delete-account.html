Delete your Stashlo account

Stashlo Ltd — loyalty wallet & rewards. This page explains how to delete your Stashlo account and the data linked to it.

Option 1 — Delete instantly in the app (fastest)

  1. Open the Stashlo app and sign in
  2. Go to the Me tab
  3. Tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm — your account and data are removed straight away

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you can't access the app, email us from the address on your account and we'll delete it for you:

Email a deletion request

Send from the email registered to your account so we can verify it's you. We complete verified requests within 30 days.

What gets deleted

Account & profileYour name, email, phone, address, loyalty ID and login are permanently removed.
Loyalty & activityYour points balances, loyalty cards, receipts, orders and requests are deleted.
Location dataAny saved collection postcode/location is removed.

What we may keep (and for how long)

We may retain a limited amount of data where the law requires it — for example, basic transaction records needed for tax, accounting or fraud-prevention. This is kept only as long as legally required and is not used for any other purpose. Everything else is deleted.

How long it takes

In-app deletion is immediate. Email requests are completed within 30 days of us verifying your identity.
